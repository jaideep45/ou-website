Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13th July 2025

1UP ("we", "our", "us") is a social habit-building app designed to help you grow alongside your friends. We care about your privacy and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the 1UP app, we collect:

a) Personal Information

  • Name – to identify you to friends
  • Phone Number – used for login and friend connections
  • Contacts – with your permission, to connect you with friends who also use 1UP

b) Habit and Journal Data

  • Habits you create, track, and complete
  • Streaks and progress
  • Journal entries, including photos you optionally upload

c) Device & Usage Data

  • Basic analytics (e.g., device type, app version) to improve performance

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Track your habits and journal entries
  • Connect you with friends on the app
  • Show your habit progress, streaks, and updates to your connected friends
  • Improve the app's experience and features

3. What Your Friends Can See

1UP is designed to be social. By using the app, you agree that your habit progress, streaks, and activity updates (e.g., when you 1UP a habit, complete a streak, or update your journal) may be visible to your friends who are connected with you inside the app.

You have control over what you post in your journal and what you share. We do not share private contact information (e.g., phone number) with other users.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data.

We may share data:

  • With your friends on the app (see above)
  • With trusted service providers who help us run and improve the app (under confidentiality agreements)
  • If required by law

5. Data Security

We store data securely using industry-standard encryption and safeguards. While no system is perfectly secure, we are committed to protecting your information.

6. Your Rights & Controls

You can:

  • Edit or delete your journal entries and habits anytime
  • Revoke contacts permission from your device settings
  • Delete your account and data by contacting us at helloo1up@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

1UP is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you in-app if we make significant changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns, reach out to:

Email: helloo1up@gmail.com
